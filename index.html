My Personal List

My Top 10 Favorite Movies

Top 1 – 5

1
The Dark Knight
2008 · Christopher Nolan
Batman faces the Joker, a criminal mastermind who plunges Gotham into anarchy in this gripping superhero epic.
2
The Shawshank Redemption
1994 · Frank Darabont
A wrongfully imprisoned banker forms an unlikely friendship and holds onto hope inside one of America's most brutal prisons.
3
Interstellar
2014 · Christopher Nolan
Astronauts travel through a wormhole near Saturn searching for a new home for humanity, blending hard science with deep emotion.
4
Pulp Fiction
1994 · Quentin Tarantino
Interconnected stories of crime, redemption, and dark comedy weave through the LA underworld in Tarantino's genre-defining masterpiece.
5
Inception
2010 · Christopher Nolan
A skilled thief enters the subconscious of others to plant an idea, navigating layers of dreams in a visually stunning heist thriller.

Top 6 – 10

6
Forrest Gump
1994 · Robert Zemeckis
A kind-hearted man from Alabama unwittingly influences several historical events while searching for his one true love.
7
The Matrix
1999 · The Wachowskis
A computer hacker discovers reality is a simulation controlled by machines and joins a rebellion that redefined sci-fi action.
8
Schindler's List
1993 · Steven Spielberg
A German businessman risks everything to save over a thousand Jewish refugees during the Holocaust in Spielberg's devastating masterwork.
9
Gladiator
2000 · Ridley Scott
A betrayed Roman general becomes a gladiator and fights his way back to exact vengeance against the corrupt emperor who murdered his family.
10
Avengers: Endgame
2019 · Russo Brothers
Earth's mightiest heroes make one final stand against Thanos in an epic, emotionally charged conclusion to Marvel's Infinity Saga.
